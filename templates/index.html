The Teacher's Journey

STEP 01

Secure Login

Start Your session with High-Security Authentication portal. Your data is encrypted and synced across all your devices.

STEP 02

Interactive Dashboard

Manage your subjects, Attendance records and take attendance through audio and imaging.

STEP 03

Course Management

Creating a new subject is easy! Just enter the name and create a subject code. ClassVision generates the QR-Code to invite students.

STEP 04

FaceID Attendance

Use high speed Computer Vision to Analyze faces and mark attendance records.

STEP 05

VoiceID Attendance

Switch to voice mode for an efficient roll call. Students speak sequentially, and out AI matches their unique voice signatures

STEP 06

Actionable Records

Review and Manage past attendance logs and view confidence scores
